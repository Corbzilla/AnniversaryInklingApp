About Anniversary Inkling

Hello! Welcome to Anniversary Inkling, a web app which helps you come up with some anniversary gift ideas for your upcoming anniversary.


How does it do that? Well start by heading back to our main page and filling in your anniversary date as well as checking the box for whether you are buying for a man, a women or check both to get more ideas. Click on Get Ideas! and violia you will be re-directed to our ideas page which will show you a list of anniversary gift ideas. This list will be based on both traditional and modern gift ideas for anniversary gifts. Protip: Click refresh in the right side of the header bar next to where it says “Ideas for you x year anniversary” to get a different set of anniversary gift ideas for your year of anniversary.


So is that all I can do here you may be asking yourself? Not at all! You’ll notice the “Add To List” button on each of the anniversary gift ideas in the list. You can register with us by providing an email address and a password. Once registered you’ll be able to create a list of anniversary gift ideas of your own by either clicking that “Add To List” button or adding your own idea using the “Add to List” form you will see after registering with us. You can then search Amazon for products based on the ideas in your list.


Ok, so what’s next? Well we have Android and iPhone apps on the way but if you don’t want to wait to take advantage of using your list on mobile, the web app is optimized for use on mobiles or tablets. So you can add anniversary gift ideas on the go or use your list to go out and check a found item in store. Also, coming soon, you’ll notice the “Search Locally” button which will provide you with a list of items in your local area! We are also working hard to add the ability to use your phones camera to take a picture to use as a reference for an anniversary gift idea. As well, we are always tweaking our idea generators to come up with more ideas and because the app uses what people are viewing most often to refine the list of anniversary ideas with newer and more popular items.
